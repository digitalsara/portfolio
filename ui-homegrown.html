HomeGrown App

UI/UX

HomeGrown is a chore assignment app,  specially curated for children.

Through a simple, easy to follow interface and fun colours, this app allows children to be rewarded in an instant for being responsible around the house. Parents benefit from being able to manage chores in a breeze, all from their separate accounts.

The app features

Integrated message board

No more sticky notes on the fridge!

Interactive Score Bank

A rewarding system to keep track of achievement points, and to teach responsibility to the little ones.

Synced Chores

Assign chores from a parent’s account and sync the across all devices.

Locate Your Loved Ones

Be aware of everyone’s location by using this easy kid-friendly interface.
