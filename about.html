How do I identify myself as a graphic designer?

“I undertake every project firstly by analyzing the challenge, then creating innovative, purposeful and accessible solutions.

Artist statement

Design surrounds us all—whether we are conscious of it or not—and my purpose as a graphic designer is to bring clarity to chaos. With technology at everyone's fingertips, it is imperative that it becomes more and more accessible. Excellent legibility, usability, and practicality are my top priorities.

My interest in beautifully rendered graphics and a world filled with colour quickly became a dream during my early adult life. Once introduced to user experience, I discovered that it is indeed the design thinking process that fascinates me the most. I undertake every project firstly by analyzing the challenge, then creating innovative, purposeful and accessible solutions.

Sara Habel
