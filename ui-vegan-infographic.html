Audience

Health conscious young adults.

Insight

Static infographics are saturating the market. When conveying complex information, printed infographics can overwhelm the viewers before they can read the full piece.

Solution

By merging it with the online world—using instances such as click, hover and scroll on any devices—users are able to interact with the content. By choosing their direction with the given information, the content becomes personalized to their unique interests.

Behind the design

Through the dissection of the subject of veganism, this infographic took a life of its own as the lines connected into shapes, and the shapes became our friendly carrot guide. Designed as two dimensional—or flat-style—the illustrations are kept simple to focus on the key talking points about a vegan diet. Earth tones accented with warm orange shades were chosen to keep the design organic. The sans-serif, handwritten typeface brings playfulness to the design, and is displayed strictly on titles. For longer paragraphs of text, a slab-serif font is used for optimized legibility.

Making it interactive

When revamping the effectiveness of this static infographic, it was imperative to use the magic of motion graphics. Implementing 3D spaces, index positions, parallax effects and smoothing transitions through the graph editor were used to achieve this interactive infographic mock-up.
